Our Process

Our process to create your dream home

1

CHOOSE YOUR CASA PODS

Browse through our selection of LoftyPods and choose the one that best fits your needs. One of our team members is always available to answer ant question you have and to walk you through the selection process.

2

ZONING & PERMITS

Once you have chosen your CasaPod you will then need to contact your municipality to verify if you will need a permit. It will be your responsibility to obtain the permit if it is required by your municipality.Use a Urban Planner to obtian permit fast

3

CHOOSE YOUR EXTERIOR SIDING

Now that the permits have been verified you can choose your homes siding material. There are several types of materials to choose from and a variety of colors and textures allowing you to create your own distinct style.

4

SELECTING YOUR FOUNDATION

A foundation plays a key role in the construction of any home and every building needs one. Your foundation provides a solid, secure platform that will ensure the weight of your modular home is properly distributed and keeps it safe from ground movement and moisture.There are several types of foundations to chooose.

5

FINAL COST & PROPOSAL

Now that you have chosen the foundation and siding options we can provided you with a final proposal and price for your pod which will include: Construction of your pod and Delivery to the site Installation.Once you accept the proposal we will provide you with your schedule of payments which includes: 35 percent deposit

7

CONTRACT

The next step is to sign the contract for your new modular pod. Our contracts are very detailed and we recommend that you take the time to read it. Make sure to review your design model options, siding and color selections and ask us any questions you may have. Feel free to have the contract reviewed by your legal counsel or other third party.

6

EXCAVATION & SITE PREPARATION

The greatest thing about modular construction is that the site preparation is done simultaneously with the construction of your home.

8

TRANSPORTATION & DELIVERY

Our team will deliver the pod to your location. The quote includes transportation cost also.You dont have to be around while we bring the pod to land. Once its done our team will contact you.

9

INSTALLATION

Our Experienced team will install your dream home on the land.And our internal security team to install a security package on your house. Your presence is needed while doing the process.
